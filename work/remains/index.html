Remains

styling - communication lead

for Remains’ first release, the Tee 1.0 drop, i styled the photoshoots and helped shape the visual language of the brand. i worked on brand recognition and communication, setting the tone for how Remains would speak and be seen. beyond styling, i supported creative direction decisions, making sure the imagery, words, and strategy connected back to the brutalist, minimalistic core of the project.
